Rules

Rules are the magic that keeps our world fun, fair, respectful and thriving.

World Rules

1-Do not destroy natural structures, ruined portals are an exception.

2-Do not leave floating trees.

3-Keep all bodies of water accessible.

4-Keep all stores in the market, any stores outside of the market will be removed.

5-The exclusion zone has no rules, except for the cheat rules.

6-PvP combat does not require consent on PvP Island.

7-If another player breaks a rule that affects you, feel free to report it staff will look into it and may issue a fine or ban.

Building Rules

1-Large farms/mob farms are required to be underground.

2-Building on the ocean requires admin permission and a [ocean build permit] doing so will result in a fine.

(Bridges must have a 2 block minimum space under them.)

3-Do not build within 10 blocks of a property marked structure unless given permission.

4-Your builds must not exceed a 20x20 radius unless you have a [Build scale permit] doing so will result in a fine.

5-Underground bases must have an above ground indicator.

6-Players are allowed a second structure, secondary structures cannot have any permits applied to them..

(The maximum structure per player/team limit is 2.)

Fines

1-Owners of a property that violates a permit's restriction will be fined 5 copper coins a week until the issue is resolved.

Property Rules

1-Property is a structure, pet, item made, tamed, or collected by the player.

2-Theft and/or destruction of another's property is bannable and is an act of PVP death combat consent unless there is no property marker.

3-Ignoring three requests to leave another player's property is giving consent to PVP.

4-Farms and Spawners are for public use, unless stated on a marked sign.

5-Any permits must be at the entrance of your property, placed in a barrel or item frame.

6-You will need admin permission to make a Community Structure.

Claiming property

1-Use a sign with your player name to mark your property, This is called a property marker.

2-Property markers must be visible from the entrance of your property.

3-You only own your marked structures and the land within a 10-block radius of the structure.

4-Any player-made structure without a property marker can be claimed by anyone.

5-You cannot claim any natural structure.

6-A team base is considered as a first base for all team members.

Rule Boards

1-Use a rule board to define the rules on your property if needed..

2-Rule boards must be visible from the entrance of your property.

3-Rule boards can ban players from your property, not staff.

4-Rule boards can remove server rules from your property only with admin permission.

5-Rule boards can add new rules to your property only with admin permission.

Pvp Rules

1-You may initiate PVP with players with 5+ hearts.

2-PVP must cease upon one party reaching 5 hearts.

3-Killing in PVP requires consent before starting, except when told to leave a property.

4-Any form of theft or property damage is an act of PVP death combat consent.

Cheat rules

1-Any external modifications made to your Minecraft client that give you an unfair advantage over another player is considered cheating and will not be tolerated.
