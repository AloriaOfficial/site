Rules

Rules are the magic that keeps our world fun, fair, respectful and thriving.

Section 1

1-Do not destroy natural structures.

2-Replant trees.

3-No floating trees.

4-Building on the ocean requires admin permission.

5-Keep all bodies of water accessible,

(bridges must have a 2.5 block minimum space under them).

Section 2

1-You're accountable for your actions against any property.

2-Property is a structure, pet, item made, tamed, or collected by the player.

3-Theft and destruction of another's property is strictly prohibited.

4-PVP without consent is strictly prohibited,

any form of theft or damage to property is an act of PVP consent.

5-Ignoring three requests to leave another player's property is giving consent to PVP.

Section 3

1-Use a sign with your player name to mark your property.(property marker)

2-You only own your marked structures and the land within a 10-block radius.

3-Farms and Spawners are for public use, unless stated on a marked sign.

4-Your property/items are yours even if it is misplaced or picked up by another player.

5-players property will be auctioned after a month of inactivity

(Dependent on circumstance).

Section 4

1-Banned players' property will be auctioned,

(Not applicable to temp-banned players).

2-No auto clickers.

3-Do not use free cam.

4-Do not use a hacked client.

5-X-Ray is Strictly prohibited.

Section 5

1-Use a rule board to define the rules on your property if needed.

2-Rule boards and property markers must be visible from the entrance of your property.

3-Rule boards can ban players from your property.

4-Rule boards can remove server rules from your property with admin permission.

5-Rule boards can add any new rules to your property

(Within reason).

Section 6

1-large farms must be placed underground.

2-If you would like you may kill and guard a thief's soul and reclaim only the stolen items.

3-Sky bases must not be located over another player's bases.

4-You as the player are responsible for reading and understanding these rules.

5-If you do not understand a rule please open a ticket before applying,

we will do our best to help you understand.
